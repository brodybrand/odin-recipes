Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tortilla
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat up pan with butter
  2. Place tortilla in pan and sprinkle shredded cheese on one half of the tortilla. Fold the empty half of the tortilla onto the cheesy side.
  3. After one side is crisped, flip the tortilla and crisp the second side. Voila
